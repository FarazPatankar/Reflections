Q)What happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?

Q)How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?
A)Staging area is different as it is an intermediate area between the working directory and the repository. It is important as it bundles its contents into a single commit and adds them to the repository.
